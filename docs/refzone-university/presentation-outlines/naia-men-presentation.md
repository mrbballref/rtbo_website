# NAIA MEN RefZone University Presentation

## Slide 1: NAIA MEN
- Claim: NAIA MEN gives officials a structured RefZone University path from weekly concept to visible game behavior.
- Overview: This track is designed for small-college men’s basketball officials preparing for high physicality, national tournament intensity, advanced post play, and mature game management. Students in this track study the full annual officiating cycle through classroom lecture, court mechanics, film laboratory, role-play, live practicum, mentoring, written exams, oral defense, and advancement board review. The teaching emphasis throughout the year is to train officials to manage small-college men’s basketball without over-officiating or losing safety standards.
- Visual: `frontend/public/assets/images/refzone/course-covers/naia-men.svg`

## Slide 2: Course Architecture
- Degree Path: Master of Science in Collegiate Basketball Officiating - Small College Men’s Concentration
- Academic Level: Master Year 1
- Proof object: 52-week course progression, seven academic day types, required evidence trail.

## Slide 3: Evidence Loop
- Lecture -> rules reasoning -> court lab -> film lab -> role-play -> live practicum -> mentor reflection.
- Visual: seven lesson visual templates from `frontend/public/assets/images/refzone/lesson-visuals/`.

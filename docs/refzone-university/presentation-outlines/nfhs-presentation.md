# NFHS RefZone University Presentation

## Slide 1: NFHS
- Claim: NFHS gives officials a structured RefZone University path from weekly concept to visible game behavior.
- Overview: The NFHS track prepares high-school basketball officials to serve education-based athletics with rules accuracy, professional communication, strong mechanics, student-athlete safety, and state-association readiness.
- Visual: `frontend/public/assets/images/refzone/course-covers/nfhs.svg`

## Slide 2: Course Architecture
- Degree Path: Certificate in High School Basketball Officiating - NFHS Track
- Academic Level: High School Foundation / NFHS Rules
- Proof object: 52-week course progression, seven academic day types, required evidence trail.

## Slide 3: Evidence Loop
- Lecture -> rules reasoning -> court lab -> film lab -> role-play -> live practicum -> mentor reflection.
- Visual: seven lesson visual templates from `frontend/public/assets/images/refzone/lesson-visuals/`.
